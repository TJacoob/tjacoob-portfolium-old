About Me

Hello, my name is Tomás, but I go by my second name most of the time, so call me Jacob. I am currently finishing my MSc degree in Information Systems and Computer Engineering, with a major in Interaction and Visualization at Instituto Superior Técnico in Lisbon.

I like to call myself an Aspiring Web Developer and hope to make a career out of designing websites and everything web related. So far I’ve done some freelancing work and other projects for my courses and extra curricular activities.

As of now, I am 23 years old and live in Oeiras, on the outskirts of Lisbon. I am a huge fan of music and concerts, and we can talk about it over a beer or coffee. I am also passionate about traveling and I hope to be able to travel over the world.
